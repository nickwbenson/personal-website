monthly playlists

every month, i have been making a playlist of my songs of the moment: both new jams and old favorites. i'm mostly trying to create little musical time capsules. i spend so much time listening to music, sometimes my library can feel like a revolving door. one second i'm obsessed, and the next it's forgotten. in an effort to fight against that, i'm hoping to look back on these in the years to come and remember songs that would otherwise be buried.
