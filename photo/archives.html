archives

these are selected archives from 2007-2019. they don't necessarily belong in any specific project or body of work, but i wanted to share them regardless.

the photos here have been shot on any number of different films using various 35mm, medium format & large format cameras. most have been printed in the darkroom and digitized, and a couple have made their way straight from film into the computer.
